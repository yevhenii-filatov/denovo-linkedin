Last night, Literacy Instruction for Texas (LIFT) and Aberg Center for Literacy jointly announced that we will be merging effective July 1st in order to offer expanded literacy services for families and adults in Dallas. The new organization will offer a Cradle to Career Continuum that will prepare preschoolers for formal education and ready adults for further education, while developing life skills needed for the navigation of daily challenges.
